Ireland

Ireland entices billionaires with robust economic prospects, strong legal protections, and opulent lifestyles. As Europe's fastest-growing economy,
it features hubs like Dublin (tech giants like Google/Meta and fintech), Cork (pharma/biotech), and Limerick (innovation and manufacturing), bolstered by
solid IP laws and access to EU single market. Tax perks include non-domiciled remittance basis, exempting foreign income/gains if not brought into Ireland,
plus tools like trusts and foreign tax credits to minimize liabilities. Elite enclaves span Dublin's Ballsbridge, Donnybrook, and Rathgar,
countryside estates in Kildare or Wicklow, or Ashford Castle retreats, with premium security, private aviation, and top-tier education/healthcare.
