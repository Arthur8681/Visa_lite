Taiwan

Taiwan entices billionaires with robust economic prospects, strong legal protections, and opulent lifestyles. As Asia's innovation powerhouse and AI hub,
it features centers like Taipei (finance and fintech), Hsinchu Science Park (semiconductors and tech), and Taichung (manufacturing and innovation),
bolstered by solid IP laws and access to global supply chains. Tax perks include a five-year 50% deduction on salary income exceeding NT$3M for
Gold Card holders, foreign tax credits, and incentives for immigrant investors via remittance-based taxation on non-Taiwan sourced income.
