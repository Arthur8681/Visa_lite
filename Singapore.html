Singapore

Singapore entices billionaires with robust economic prospects, strong legal protections, and opulent lifestyles. As Asia's premier financial and innovation hub,
it features centers like the Central Business District (finance and fintech), one-north (tech, biotech, and media), and Marina Bay (startups and digital innovation),
bolstered by solid IP laws and access to ASEAN and global markets. Tax perks include territorial taxation exempting foreign income if not remitted,
no capital gains or inheritance taxes, and progressive personal rates up to 24% with reliefs for high earners. Elite enclaves span Nassim Road (Billionaire's Row),
Bukit Timah estates, Sentosa Cove waterfronts, and Orchard Road luxury developments, with premium security, private aviation,
and world-class education/healthcare.
