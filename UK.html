United Kingdom

The UK entices billionaires with robust economic prospects, strong legal protections, and opulent lifestyles. As a global financial powerhouse,
it features hubs like London (finance and fintech), Cambridge (tech innovation), and emerging AI zones in the North East, bolstered by solid IP laws
and access to European markets.Tax benefits include the 4-year Foreign Income and Gains regime, exempting foreign earnings for new residents,
plus wealth tools like trusts and deductions. Elite enclaves span Mayfair and Kensington in London, Windsor estates, or Cotswolds retreats,
with premium security, private aviation, and top-tier education/healthcare.
