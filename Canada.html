Canada

Canada entices billionaires with robust economic prospects, strong legal protections, and opulent lifestyles. As a G7 powerhouse,
it features hubs like Toronto (finance and tech), Vancouver (tech and film), Calgary (energy), and Montreal (AI and aerospace),
bolstered by solid IP laws and USMCA access to North American markets.Tax perks include foreign tax credits to avoid double taxation, R&D incentives,
and wealth tools like trusts, though residents face worldwide income taxation. Elite enclaves span Toronto's Bridle Path and Rosedale,
Vancouver's Shaughnessy, Whistler retreats, or Muskoka estates, with premium security, private aviation, and top-tier education/universal healthcare.
