New Zealand

New Zealand entices billionaires with robust economic prospects, strong legal protections, and opulent lifestyles. As a dynamic Asia-Pacific economy,
it features hubs like Auckland (tech and finance), Christchurch (innovation and biotech), and Wellington (government and startups),
bolstered by solid IP laws aligned with international standards and access to regional markets.
Tax perks include a 4-year exemption on foreign-sourced income for new residents, plus tools like trusts and
the new Revenue Account Method offering a 30% discount on foreign investment gains.
